Mozilla Thunderbird
  1. From the Tools menu, click Account Settings.
  2. From the Account Settings screen, click Add Account
  3. Select Email account and click Next
  4. Enter your name and email address and click Next
  5. Select IMAP as the incoming server type.
  6. Enter the Incoming Server, enter mail.mailconfig.net
  7. Check Use Global Inbox to store mail locally.
  8. Enter the Outgoing Server, enter mail.mailconfig.net
  9. Click Next.
  10. Enter your full email address as the Incoming User Name and click Next
  11. Enter an Account Name and click Next
  12. Confirm the information is correct and click Finish
  13. Select Outgoing Server (SMTP)
  14. Change the port to 587
  15. Enable the checkbox Use name and password.
  16. Enter your full email address as the username.
  17. Set Use secure connection to TLS
