Blackberry
  1. Select model and follow step-by-step instructions
  2. Click the Blackberry (menu) button and select Setup
  3. Click Email Settings
  4. Click Add
  5. Select Other
  6. Enter your email address and password, then click Next
  7. You will receive a confirmation message; click OK
  8. Select the newly created account
  9. Select Edit
  10. Confirm your name and signature
  11. Scroll down to Advanced Options
  12. Click Advanced Options
    Enter mail.mailconfig.net
  13. Verify the settings shown above
