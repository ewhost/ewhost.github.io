iPhone & iPad
  1. From the home screen of the iPhone, choose Settings.
  2. Select Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Select Add Account.
  4. Select Other.
  5. Enter your name, your full email address, and your password. The description is optional. Select Save at the top-right corner when finished.
  6. Select IMAP button.
  7. Under Incoming Mail Server enter mail.mailconfig.net
  8. Enter your full email address as User Name.
  9. Your password will be entered already. Scroll down.
  10. Under Outgoing Mail Server enter mail.mailconfig.net
  11. Enter your full email address as User Name and your email password.
  12. Tap the Save button and you should receive all green checkmarks
